BIOGRAPHY

Mildred Peace Ehiguese was born on the 29th of August 1993 Originating from Edo State, Nigeria. She is the first child of her parents. Mildred attended her primary school and secondary school in Success International School, Yola, Adamawa State and Concordia College, Yola, Adamawa state respectively. She then studied psychology in Redeemer's University, Ede, Osun state where she won an award from her department for reputable clergy 2014, reputable financial secretary 2015 and most fashionable female 2015, and graduated in 2016 with a second class honors degree. She served her country for a year in the National Youth Service Corps in 2017 and was posted to the the occupational therapy arm under the psychiatry department in University College Hospital, Ibadan, Oyo state. On ending her service year, Mildred was crowned the 41st Miss Nigeria on the 21st night of December 2017. Mildred is known for her sonorous voice, is reputable and has a caring nature, on the celebration of her 24th birthday she also celebrated her visitation to a handful of orphanages to give supplies and has continued the good deeds. Being an artistic and multi-talented lady, Mildred over a period of time acquired versatile skills sustainable by the entrepreneural world. Mildred was crowned the 41st Miss Nigeria on the 22nd December 2017 and is passionate about the mental health of children who have been sexually assaulted in Nigerian, Africans and the world at large. She is also passionate about the eradication of hunger among children and illiteracy and would love to provide free food, water and education for children. She started the Mild Heart Organization to meet these needs and has carried out various projects during her reign as Miss Nigeria. She has worked in collaboration with the national agency for prohibition of trafficking in persons (NAPTIP), youth empowerment and development initiative (YEDI) and other organizations to achieve remarkable events. Mildred worked on 8 successful projects during her reign as miss Nigeria, the world malaria day project with YEDI, walk against rape, NAPTIP awareness against human trafficking, the “dark Lagos” outreach with Mild Heart organisation, children day celebration with spar, 300 orphans outreach with Dunamis international gospel center, coconut tree planting with jam the coconut food company, ajegule back to school out reach and is currently a part of the 300 project by Paul Ukonu. After her reign, Mildred started three companies, NIMIL organics for the production of natural handmade spices and oils and POEM luxury and style for styling and fashion and Walk with Mildred, a pageant and etiquette coaching school.
